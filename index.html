Secret Friend

  • High Concept: Collect notes in your friends appartment and figure out what their secret is.
  • Player Experience Goals: A mystery solving experience.
  • Genre: Mystery/Puzzle solving.
  • Game play: Arrow keys control movement.
  • Prototype goal: An instrction menu. Along with sounds and random spawning.
  • Features: Having 4 different possible outcomes.
  • Setting/Premise: You have noticed something differnt about your friend lately, but can not figure out what it is. So, you decided to break into their appartment to search for clues. You have an idea of 4 possible outcomes. 1. Your friend is a murderer. 2. Your friend is a theif. 3. Your friend has no secret. 4. Your friend is dead.
  • Story: Mentioned above.
  • Target Audience: Casual/Mystery Fans.
  • Play time: About one minute.
  • Strategies: Think before you click.
